## Databricks Model Serving Acceptable Use Policy

The **Databricks Model Serving Acceptable Use Policy** is defined in the applicable model terms document for Databricks on AWS. This document sets out the terms that govern the acceptable use of models accessed through [Databricks Model Serving](/concepts/databricks-model-serving.md). ^[applicable-model-terms-databricks-on-aws.md]

The policy covers restrictions on model content, prohibited use cases, and compliance obligations for users of the service. For the full text of the policy, refer to the official documentation at [https://docs.databricks.com/aws/en/machine-learning/model-serving/acceptable-use-models](https://docs.databricks.com/aws/en/machine-learning/model-serving/acceptable-use-models). ^[applicable-model-terms-databricks-on-aws.md]
